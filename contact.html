❓ Frequently asked questions

How quickly do you reply?

We aim to respond within 24 hours, often sooner. If your question is urgent, feel free to call us during business hours.

Can I visit the garden?

Yes! Our demonstration garden is open to the public every Saturday from 10am to 4pm. We also host seasonal workshops — check our events page.

Do you offer personalized consultations?

Absolutely. We offer virtual and in-person consultations for garden design, soil restoration, and plant health. Get in touch and we'll tailor a plan for you.
