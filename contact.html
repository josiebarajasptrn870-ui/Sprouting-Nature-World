Yes! We host seasonal workshops at our cottage from April to October. Drop us a message and we’ll send you the schedule.

Can I send you a recipe idea?

Absolutely — we love hearing from fellow bakers. Share your elderberry creations and we might feature them in a future post.

We aim for 24–48 hours. If you don’t hear back, please check your spam folder or nudge us again.
