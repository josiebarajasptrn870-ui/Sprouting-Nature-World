Rooted in

Zelvora Plant Mosaic was born from a single question: how can we garden with deeper awareness? We are a small, independent team of horticulturists, soil ecologists, and storytellers who believe that every garden is a living mosaic — of species, seasons, and human hands.

✦ Our compass

Soil first

We champion regenerative practices: no‑dig, cover crops, and fungal networks. Healthy soil grows resilient plants.

Evidence‑based

Every guide is cross‑referenced with agricultural research, historical wisdom, and on‑ground trials from our own plots.

Community mosaic

We believe in shared knowledge — from urban balconies to rural homesteads. Gardening is a collective craft.

Slow & seasonal

We resist the fast‑garden trend. Our approach follows the rhythm of the land, not the calendar of convenience.

⏳ Our story

2018 · The first plot

We started as a small community garden in zone 6, experimenting with heirloom varieties and compost systems.

2020 · Digital roots

Launched the Zelvora journal to share our observations — soil tests, pest cycles, and crop successes.

2023 · Mosaic expanded

Welcomed new collaborators and broadened our focus to include native plant ecology, water conservation, and climate‑adaptive gardening.

2026 · Growing forward

Today we reach gardeners across 30+ countries, always grounded in the belief that every garden tells a story.
