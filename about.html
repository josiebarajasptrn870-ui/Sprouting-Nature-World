About Emberstone

We are a small, obsessive team of cooks, writers, and food scientists. Our mission is to demystify craft cooking — from sourdough and fermentation to fire and smoke — with precision, warmth, and a little bit of poetry.

✦ founded in 2026 · rooted in tradition, guided by science

📖 our philosophy

We believe cooking is both craft and science. Every recipe is a hypothesis, every meal a celebration. We value precision without rigidity, and tradition without dogma.

🌱 ethical kitchen

Seasonal, sustainable, and deeply respectful of ingredients. We work with small producers and always highlight the story behind each staple.
