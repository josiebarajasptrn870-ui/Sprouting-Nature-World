More than a cookbook.
We’re a kitchen library.

Eastwind Kitchen Press was founded to preserve, interpret, and celebrate the deep culinary traditions of Asia — with rigour, curiosity, and a sense of wonder.

📖 Since 2018 🌏 12 regions 🧑‍🍳 45+ contributors 🔥 300+ recipes

Our mission

To bring authentic Asian cooking into every kitchen — not through shortcuts, but through understanding. We believe that technique, ingredient knowledge, and cultural context are the true foundations of flavour.

🌾 Ingredient-first

We explore the why behind each ingredient — from heirloom rice to fermented bean pastes.

🔥 Technique matters

Knife skills, wok heat, steaming, braising — we break down every method with clarity.

🌏 Regional depth

We map flavours across China, Japan, Korea, Southeast Asia, and beyond.

📚 Always learning

Our library grows with each article, guided by chefs, farmers, and home cooks.

📅 Our journey

2018
Eastwind launched — a small newsletter sharing family recipes from Shanghai and Seoul.
2020
The library grew — we added regional deep-dives, fermentation guides, and a knife skills series.
2022
First print edition — a limited-run zine on wok technique, sold out in 3 weeks.
2024
Eastwind Kitchen Press becomes a full‑time publication with a global contributor network.
