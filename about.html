From hedgerow to hearth

a small-bakery journal rooted in wild foraging & slow fermentation

Elderberry Oven Tales began in a Somerset cottage, where the scent of woodsmoke and sourdough lingers. We believe in flour-dusted hands, seasonal fruit, and the quiet magic of baking with what the land gives.

Every recipe is shaped by the hedgerows we wander, the neighbours we break bread with, and the rhythm of the wood-fired oven.

— the old stone oven, always ready —

🌾 what we hold dear

  • Foraging with respect — elderberries, crab apples, wild plums
  • Long, cool ferments for depth and digestibility
  • Recipes that welcome both beginners and seasoned bakers
  • Community over competition — we share everything we learn
  • Every bake tells a story: of place, season, and memory
