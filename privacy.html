Privacy Policy

Your data, your kitchen. We believe in transparency, minimal collection, and zero clutter. Here’s how we handle information at Emberstone.

🔒 updated May 2026 📬 privacy@emberstone.me 📍 Brooklyn · NY

Information we collect

We collect minimal data to improve your experience and to communicate with you. This includes:

  • Contact form submissions — name, email address, and message content (only when you voluntarily reach out).
  • Newsletter signups — email address and preferred topics (if you opt in).
  • Analytics — anonymized usage data (page visits, referral sources) via privacy‑first tools; no personal identifiers.
  • Cookies — we use only essential cookies for functionality and a small number of performance cookies. See our Cookies Policy.
📦 We never sell, rent, or trade your data.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send you occasional updates, recipes, or journal posts if you’ve subscribed.
  • To improve our website and content based on aggregate trends.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

Data retention: We keep your data only as long as necessary. You can request deletion anytime at privacy@emberstone.me.

Data sharing & third parties

We share data only with trusted processors that help us operate:

  • Email delivery (via a secure platform) for newsletters and replies.
  • Hosting provider — server logs are retained for security, but are not analyzed for personal tracking.
  • Analytics — we use a self‑hosted, cookieless analytics tool that does not fingerprint users.

We do not share your data with advertisers, social media platforms, or data brokers.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., unsubscribe from emails).
  • Object to processing or request data portability.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@emberstone.me or use the contact form. We’ll respond within 30 days.

Security & integrity

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All connections are encrypted via HTTPS.
  • Access to stored data is restricted to essential personnel.
  • We regularly review our security practices.

🔐 If you have a security concern, please reach out immediately.
