Your privacy matters

how we handle your data with the same care as a slow‑rise sourdough

We believe in transparency, warmth, and never over‑proofing your trust. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

🍞 Information we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily share with us, such as:

  • Contact details — name, email address, and any message you send via our contact form.
  • Newsletter sign‑ups — if you subscribe, we store your email to send you our occasional baking notes.
  • Usage data — basic analytics (like page views) to help us understand what our readers enjoy most. We use anonymised data only.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. It stays between us, like a secret family recipe.

🕊️ How we use your data

  • To reply to your messages and inquiries.
  • To send you our newsletter (only if you’ve opted in).
  • To improve our website and content.

We process your data on the lawful basis of legitimate interest — we want to bake a better experience for you. You can opt out anytime.

🔒 Data security & retention

We take security seriously. All data is stored on secure servers and accessed only by the Elderberry Oven team. We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services or as required by law. If you’d like us to delete your data, just drop us a line.

🍪 Cookies? Just a light dusting

We use minimal cookies — only essential ones for functionality and a tiny pinch of analytics. We don’t use tracking cookies for advertising. You can manage your preferences in your browser settings. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

📬 Your rights

Under UK and EU data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccuracies.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, simply email us at hello@elderberryoven.com — we’ll respond within 30 days.

🌿 Updates to this policy

We may update this page occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We’ll always post the latest version here, and if changes are significant, we’ll highlight them.

📅 Last updated: 30 June 2026

This policy applies to all visitors and users of the Elderberry Oven Tales website.
