Privacy & data

How we handle your information — transparently and with care.

📅 Last updated: June 30, 2026 ⚖️ GDPR & CCPA compliant

1. Information we collect

Eastwind Kitchen Press collects minimal data to improve your experience and communicate with you. We may collect:

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone (if you submit a form).
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, referring URLs (via anonymized analytics).
  • Cookies — we use essential cookies for functionality and preference cookies for analytics. See our Cookies Policy.

2. How we use your data

  • To respond to inquiries, comments, or collaboration requests.
  • To send you newsletters (only if you opt in).
  • To improve our content and site performance.
  • We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

🔒 Your trust matters — we only process data that is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected.

3. Data storage & security

Your information is stored securely on servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for data transmission and access controls to protect against unauthorized access.

4. Your rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Request a copy of your data (portability).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, please use the contact form or email us directly at privacy@eastwindkitchen.com.

5. Third‑party services

We use the following third‑party services that may process data:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized traffic analysis (IP anonymization enabled).
  • Mailchimp — for newsletter delivery (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Fonts & CDN — Google Fonts and Unsplash images are loaded from external CDNs.

These services have their own privacy policies, which we recommend reviewing.

6. Cookies

We use cookies sparingly. Essential cookies are necessary for the site to function. Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our content. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via your browser settings. For full details, see our Cookies Policy.

7. Children’s privacy

Our site is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the “Last updated” date at the top.

📬 Questions? Reach out to our data protection officer at privacy@eastwindkitchen.com or via our contact page.
