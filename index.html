    ❓ Frequently asked questions

    What is the best oil for stir‑frying?

    Peanut oil or rice bran oil due to high smoke point. Avoid olive oil.

    How do I store fresh noodles?

    Refrigerate in a sealed container with a little cornstarch; use within 2 days.

    Can I substitute fish sauce?

    Use a mix of soy sauce and a touch of lime juice, but the umami will differ.
