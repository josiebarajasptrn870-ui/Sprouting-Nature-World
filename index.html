    ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the best organic fertilizer for vegetables?
    Compost and well-aged manure are foundational. For specific crops, blood meal (nitrogen) and bone meal (phosphorus) work well. Always test your soil first.
    How do I start a permaculture garden?
    Begin with observation: sun, wind, water flow. Then design zones, start small with a herb spiral, and expand gradually. Our 10-step guide covers everything.
    Can I grow vegetables in shade?
    Yes — leafy greens, roots, and some herbs thrive in partial shade. We have a dedicated guide on shade-tolerant crops.
