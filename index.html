    Frequently asked questions

    What’s the best flour for sourdough?

    We recommend a blend of bread flour and whole wheat — at least 12% protein. Our dedicated guide covers 10 flour types.

    How do I fix a broken sauce?

    Emulsify with a tablespoon of cold butter or cream, off‑heat. For vinaigrettes, add a splash of warm water.

    Can I adapt recipes for high altitude?

    Yes. Adjust liquid and leavening. See our high‑altitude baking series.
