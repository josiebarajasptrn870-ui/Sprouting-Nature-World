    Frequently asked questions

    Can I use frozen elderberries?

    Yes — they work well in most recipes. Thaw and drain excess liquid before incorporating into dough.

    How long does elderberry shrub keep?

    Refrigerated, our shrub stays vibrant for 6 months. The acidity preserves it naturally.

    Is this blog beginner-friendly?

    Absolutely. We have guides for first-timers and deep dives for experienced bakers.
