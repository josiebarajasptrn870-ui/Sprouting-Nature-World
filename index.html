    🛠 In‑depth tutorial: seed starting mix

    Custom blend for vigorous seedlings

    A professional recipe that balances aeration, moisture, and nutrition — without peat.

  • 1 part coco coir (hydrated)
  • 1 part perlite (coarse)
  • ½ part high‑quality compost (screened)
  • 2 tbsp basalt dust per gallon
  • 1 tsp kelp meal per gallon
    • IngredientFunctionAlternative
    Coco coirWater retentionLeaf mould
    PerliteDrainagePumice
    CompostNutrient baseWorm castings
    BasaltTrace mineralsGlacial rock dust

    Common mistake: over‑compacting the mix. Keep it fluffy, water from below.

    ❓ Frequently asked questions

    Can I use this mix for all vegetables?

    Yes, but adjust perlite for heavy feeders like tomatoes (add 10% more compost).

    How often should I water seedlings?

    Check daily; water when the top ½″ feels dry. Bottom watering is preferred.

    Is this mix organic?

    Absolutely — all ingredients are OMRI‑listed or natural.
