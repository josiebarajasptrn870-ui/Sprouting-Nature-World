Cookies Policy

We keep it light and transparent. Here’s how we use cookies to make your experience better — and how you stay in control.

🍪 updated May 2026 📬 privacy@emberstone.me 📍 Brooklyn · NY

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and keep the site running smoothly.

We use first‑party cookies (set by us) and minimal third‑party cookies (for analytics). We never use intrusive tracking or cross‑site profiling.

Types of cookies we use

CategoryPurposeDuration
essentialSession management, security, and basic functionality.session / persistent
preferencesRemember your settings (e.g., dark mode, language).up to 12 months
analyticsAggregate, anonymized usage stats to improve content.up to 24 months
🔹 No marketing, tracking, or third‑party ad cookies.

How you can manage cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies — though disabling essential ones may affect site functionality.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.

You can also opt out of analytics cookies by adjusting your preferences here (cookie consent tool).

Third‑party cookies

We use a self‑hosted analytics tool that does not set third‑party cookies. No data is shared with Google, Facebook, or any ad network. Our hosting provider may set a server‑side session cookie for security, but that is strictly necessary.

🛡️ We do not use fingerprinting, cross‑site tracking, or persistent identifiers for marketing.

Your consent

By continuing to use Emberstone Meal Journal, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your cookies or using the consent management tool (available on our homepage).

If you have questions, contact us at privacy@emberstone.me.
