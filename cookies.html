Cookies Policy

We use only essential, respectful cookies — like a gentle drizzle of water for your browsing experience.

Last updated: June 2026 Minimal & transparent

Our cookie philosophy

At Zelvora Plant Mosaic, we believe in a clutter-free, respectful web. We use cookies sparingly — only to enhance your experience and to understand how our community interacts with our content. We never use intrusive tracking or sell your data.

Minimal by design: We use only essential and functional cookies. No third-party advertising cookies, no cross-site tracking.

What cookies we use

We use a small set of cookies to keep things running smoothly. Below is a clear breakdown:

Name Purpose Duration Type
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preference (so we don't bother you again) 1 year Essential
session_id Maintains your session while you browse (e.g., form submissions) Session Essential
language_pref Stores your chosen language (if applicable) 6 months Functional
analytics_anon Anonymized usage data (page views, clicks) to improve our content 24 months Analytical

We use a self-hosted analytics solution — data never leaves our server and is fully anonymized.

How to manage cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, please note that blocking essential cookies may affect your experience on our site.

  • Browser settings: You can adjust your cookie preferences in your browser settings (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Look for "Privacy" or "Cookies" in the settings menu.
  • Withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or adjusting your settings. We will then ask for your preference again on your next visit.
  • Our preference panel: You can also manage your cookie choices via the small banner that appears when you first visit our site.
Your choice matters: We respect your privacy. You can always opt out of non-essential cookies. We only use what we truly need.

Third-party cookies

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or social media tracking pixels. We may include links to external resources (e.g., partner organizations), but those sites have their own cookie policies. We encourage you to review them.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. We will always post the updated version on this page with a new "last updated" date. We recommend checking back periodically.

Have questions about cookies? Feel free to reach out to us via our contact page or email privacy@zelvora.com.
