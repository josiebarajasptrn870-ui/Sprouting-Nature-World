Cookies & choices

We use cookies to keep things running smoothly and to understand how you interact with our kitchen.

🍪 Last updated: June 30, 2026 ⚙️ Manage your preferences

What are cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience. We use them sparingly and transparently.

🍪 Our approach — we only use essential cookies by default. Analytics and preference cookies are optional and require your consent.

Types of cookies we use

Below is a clear breakdown of the cookies we set and what they do.

NamePurposeTypeDuration
eastwind_session Maintains your session and site functionality. Essential Session
cookie_consent Stores your cookie preference choices. Essential 1 year
_ga (Google Analytics) Anonymized traffic analysis to improve content. Analytics 2 years
_gid (Google Analytics) Distinguishes users for session metrics. Analytics 24 hours
pref_theme Remembers your preferred display settings. Preference 6 months

Your control

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the site’s functionality. For analytics and preference cookies, we ask for your explicit consent before placing them.

Manage your consent: You can update your choices anytime by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer or by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

Third‑party cookies

We use Google Analytics (with IP anonymization) to collect anonymous usage data. These cookies are set by Google and are subject to their privacy policy. We also use external CDNs for fonts and images, which may set their own cookies — though we do not control them.

More information

If you have questions about our cookie usage, please see our Privacy Policy or contact us at privacy@eastwindkitchen.com.

📬 Prefer no cookies? You can set your browser to block all cookies, but please note that some parts of the site may not work as intended.
